About

Artist, programmer and live coding performer.

Currently available for contract work, commissions and consultancy.

I’m a generalist, able to pickup any project or programming language quickly and zone in on solving the most pressing problems. I have no believe in the one true language or programming style. I’m interested in sectors around music, art and education.

I’m spend time creating art and exploring live coding as a means of self expression and performance as Repl Electric.

I’ve contributed to many open source projects across a number of languages and have lots of open source projects of my own brewing. I have spoken at conferences all over the world covering such topics as kanban, testing, rhetoric, creative machines, Go AI, creative coding, live coding and startups.

Contact me: joe@josephwilk.net
